2. FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Q: How do I start a game?

A: Open PickleBallers on your Apple Watch, complete the quick setup (singles/doubles, game length, who serves first), then tap START GAME.

Q: Why isn't my heart rate showing?

A: Make sure HealthKit permissions are enabled. On your Apple Watch go to Settings → Privacy & Security → Health → PickleBallers and turn all permissions ON.

Q: How do I undo a point?

A: Swipe left from the main scoring screen to reach the Undo page, then tap Undo.

Q: Where is my game history?

A: Open the PickleBallers app on your iPhone and tap the History tab.

Q: How do I cancel my subscription?

A: Open the Settings app on your iPhone → tap your name → Subscriptions → PickleBallers → Cancel Subscription.

Q: My workout isn't saving to Apple Health.

A: Make sure you tap START GAME on the Watch before playing. The workout saves automatically when you end the game.
